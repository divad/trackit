Your Forge Password

Below is an alternative password you can use to access your git, subversion or mercurial repositories.

When working with a repository you are prompted to enter your University password repeatedly which becomes tiresome. Whilst the Subversion client can 'save' your password it is stored in plain text and often unprotected. The git client can 'cache' or 'save' as well, both of which are stored in plain text. Mercurial supports a non-standard keyring extension, otherwise you have to store your password in plain text.

Instead of using your university password you can use an alternative Forge-only password. You can safely store this password when your client prompts you to do so safe in the knowledge that your University password is not being saved. If you believe this password has been compromised you can reset it by clicking 'Regenerate' below.
