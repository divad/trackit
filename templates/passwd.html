Your Subversion Password

Below is an alternative password you can use to access your subversion repositories.

When working with a subversion repository you are prompted to enter your University password repeatedly which becomes tiresome. Whilst the Subversion client can 'save' your password it is stored in plain text and often unprotected. Instead of using your university password you can use an alternative Subversion-only password. You can safely store this password when Subversion prompts you to do so safe in the knowledge that your University password is not being saved. If you believe your Subversion password has been compromised you can reset it by clicking 'Regenerate' below.
